IIIE CRCE

Earthquake Proof Building

The objective of the competition is to create a structure out of the supplied materials that can withstand the simulated earthquake it would be subjected to. • Participants would have to build a multistorey structure that would be subjected to testing on a machine that can simulate an earthquake. • The person/team whose structure can withstand the test for the longest duration would be declared winner. The materials provided could include: Rubber bands, Wooden sticks, Thread, Clay, Lego, Thermocol etc. After the designated time limit is over the structures built by each team shall be subjected to a load not exceeding 250gm. The structures will then be placed on a vibrating platform and the building that survives the longest without falling will be declared the winning design.
